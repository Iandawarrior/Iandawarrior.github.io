Techreative

Nov '19 to Present
This role was really my bread and butter when it came to paving my career path as I moved from a young bright-eyed junior dev into a foundationally strong, industry battle-tested veteran. I have used so many technologies over the years that it would be hard to list them all, but some of the more prominent ones in no particular order would be React, Vue, Svelte, Django, Node.js, Meteor.js, Next.js, Amazon Web Services, Docker.
